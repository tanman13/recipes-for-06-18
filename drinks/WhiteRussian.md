White Russian
===============

# Ingredients
- 1 part Vodka
- 2 parts Milk or light cream

# Supplies
- Shot glass
- Rock glass

# Instructions
1. Open vodka bottle.
2. Pour vodka into shot glass.
3. Close vodka bottle.
4. Pour shot glass of vodka into other glass 
5. Stir in milk/cream.
6.  Drink.
